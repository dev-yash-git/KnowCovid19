Are there any serious side effect of vaccines?
Although rare, some people have experienced more serious short-term side effects after getting a COVID-19 vaccine. These side effects include immediate allergic reactions and a severe type of allergic reaction called anaphylaxis.
What is Black Fungus?
Black Fungus, usually infects people whose immune system has been compromised, causing blackening or discolouration over the nose, blurred or double vision, chest pain, breathing difficulties and coughing blood. It is not Contagious.
What are the chances of getting Covid-19 after vaccine?
According to medical experts, cases where fully vaccinated individuals test positive for coronavirus are possible, but have so far been rare.While the vaccine itself cannot give you the virus, it is also not 100% effective at preventing the virus entirely, though those who receive the vaccine are far less likely to be hospitalized or die from it
Why is India facing oxygen shortage?
India is producing enough oxygen, at just over 7,000 tons a day. Most is for industrial use but can be diverted for medical purposes. The Indian government has now directed most of the country's supply of industrially produced oxygen toward the health care system.However, most oxygen producers are based in India's east, while the soaring demand has been in cities in the western and northern parts of the country.
